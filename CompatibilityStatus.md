## Validation Policy

- Fast checks stay sequential: build, focused unit test classes, and selected smoke tests.
- Heavy compatibility media runs and multi-million-cycle true-drive serial-load milestones stay opt-in through environment variables and local untracked manifests under `C64/DISKS`.
- ROMs, commercial media, protected images, screenshots, and generated binary artifacts must remain untracked unless explicitly approved.
- Latest focused Phase 3 work covers VIC low-phase refresh/idle reads, late-start bad-line unstable startup data, cycle-55/56 sprite DMA enable/Y-match latching, continued sprite DMA across body rows after the initial Y compare, latched sprite BA warning behavior, cycle-58 sprite display latching, rasterline-wrapped sprite DMA middle-byte tracing, and the cycle-15 `$D017` sprite MCBASE crunch formula; external FLI/open-border/sprite-multiplex demo corpus proof is still required before calling Phase 3 complete.

## Near-Term Milestones

1. Extend machine/chip profiles with additional board/chip revisions and deeper 1541-family hardware differences.
2. Convert the local compatibility manifest into a reusable corpus runner with deterministic pass/fail reasons and stable aggregate reporting.
3. Close VIC-II timing gaps that block raster splits, sprite multiplexing, and protected loaders.
4. Expand 1541 low-level media support for protected G64 behavior beyond first-stage write-splice/erase behavior and toward persistent low-level format support.
5. Extend the cartridge framework with common fastload/freezer formats, bank switching, expansion I/O, and REU hooks.
